The subjects

Three classes are addressed in this STEM: chemistry, biology, and physics. The content taught in each of these classes is difficult and sometimes hard to understand because it is taught in English, but the classes are well-studied and students learn a lot of specialized English.



Stem Project

This field called Stem Project will be done as a research project during the summer vacation. You will study what you want to know more about by yourself and put it in a poster. They will also present it after the summer vacation, so they can acquire a lot of skills through this activity.
